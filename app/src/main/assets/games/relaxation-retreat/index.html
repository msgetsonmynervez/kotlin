Relaxation Retreat

Welcome to your tropical island game paradise! Pick a game below to unwind, challenge your mind, and enjoy some island vibes. Each game has its own fun tropical music!

🔢

Island Sudoku

Classic 9×9 number puzzle. Fill every row, column, and 3×3 box with 1 through 9.

Tap an empty cell, type a number. Press "Check Solution" to see your progress.

📝

MS Crossword

A crossword featuring Multiple Sclerosis and nervous system terms. New puzzle each time!

Read the clues, type letters. Press "Check Words" when you're done.

💎

Tropical Match-3

Swap colorful island tiles to line up 3 or more. They disappear and new ones fall in!

Tap one tile, then tap a neighbor to swap. Match 3 or more to score!

🃏

Island Solitaire

Classic Klondike solitaire. Stack cards in alternating colors, build suits on foundations.

Tap a card to select (glows gold), then tap where to move it.

🔤

Health Wordle

Guess a secret 5-letter health word in 6 tries. Green = right spot. Yellow = wrong spot.

Type a word, press Enter. Use color clues to narrow it down!

🧠

Nervous System Trivia

Five fun questions about the human nervous system. See how many you get right!

Tap the answer you think is correct. Score shown at the end.
